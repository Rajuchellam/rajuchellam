Raju Chellam is a Member of the Singapore NCCAC (National Cloud Computing Advisory Council) under IMDA (Infocomm Development Authority) and Deputy Chair of the COIR (Cloud Outage Incidence Response) Group under ITSC (IT Standards Committee). He is also Hon Vice Chairman of the Cloud and Data Chapter at SGTech, previously called SiTF (Singapore IT Federation) and Hon Secretary of the Cloud Chapter of SCS (Singapore Computer Society). He has been a Past President of the BCG (Business Continuity Group) at SCS. Raju is a Senior Adviser at the CIO Academy Asia, Co-Chair of the CIR (Cloud Incidence Response) at CSA (Cloud Security Alliance) and member of the APEC DARE (Data Analytics Raising Employment) Panel. He was conferred as an SCS Fellow in March 2018.

Raju is currently Vice President of New Technologies at Fusionex International, a leading software solutions firm that specializes in Big Data Analytics, IoT, AI and Machine Learning. Raju has been in the IT industry for the last 35 years. Prior to joining Fusionex, he was Head of Big Data and Cloud – as well as Healthcare and Gov – for Dell South Asia region.

Prior to joining Dell, he was Managing Director of TechTrenders Asia, a boutique management advisory firm that helped companies navigate new technologies in virtualization, DC optimization and migrating legacy apps to a cloud computing platform. He has previously worked in Hewlett-Packard (APJ Director of Communications), AMI Partners Inc (APAC Vice President) and as the BizIT Editor of The Business Times, Singapore, for a decade.
